Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Security

How do I verify my account?
Follow our simple KYC process: upload a valid ID, proof of address, and complete the verification form.
How to enable 2FA?
Go to Security Settings, select "Enable 2FA", and follow the steps to set up using Google Authenticator.

Deposits & Withdrawals

How long do PIX transfers take?
PIX transfers are instant! Once you initiate the transfer, funds should appear in your account within seconds.
What are the withdrawal limits?
Withdrawal limits vary by verification level. Basic accounts can withdraw up to 1,000 USDC daily.
