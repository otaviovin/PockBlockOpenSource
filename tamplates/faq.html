Frequently Asked Questions

Account & Security

How do I create my account?
Go to login page and click on Create account button, wait a moment and receive your account information.
How to login in my account?
Go to login page, fill with your secret key or pass phrase, wait a moment and the main page will open.

Deposits & Withdrawals

How long do PIX transfers take?
PIX transfers are instant! Once you initiate the transfer, funds should appear in your account within seconds.
How can I got my withdraw?
Withdrawal limits vary by verification level. Basic accounts can withdraw up to 1,000 USDC daily.
